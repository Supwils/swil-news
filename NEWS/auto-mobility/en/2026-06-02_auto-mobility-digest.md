### 3. BYD May sales edge up; exports +80% YoY; "God's Eye" liability guarantee for ADAS accidents (OEM / China)

### 5. Lucid recalls 2,039 Air Pure sedans over Gen 4 inverter drive-loss risk (Safety / U.S.)
**Summary:** InsideEVs reported June 2, 2026, that Lucid is recalling 2,039 rear-drive Air Pure vehicles (2023–2025) because Gen 4 inverters (built Sep 13, 2023–Dec 12, 2024) may fail under motor vibration, causing sudden loss of motive power.
