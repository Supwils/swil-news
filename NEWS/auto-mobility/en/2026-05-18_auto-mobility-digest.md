### 4. XPeng rolls its first mass-produced, full-stack robotaxi off the line in Guangzhou (Autonomous mobility)
**Summary:** Electrek reported on **May 18, 2026** that XPeng produced the first mass-manufactured unit of its **robotaxi** in Guangzhou, claiming a **China-first** milestone for a **fully in-house, full-stack** robotaxi program. XPeng targets **pilot operations in H2 2026** and aims for **fully autonomous on-site operations by early 2027**, subject to validation and regulatory approvals.
