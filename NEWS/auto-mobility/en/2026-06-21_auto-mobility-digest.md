### 1. China's "Reverse Market": ICE Prices Collapse While NEV Brands Tighten Discounts (Market / China)
**Summary:** The Paper reported on June 21, 2026 that China's auto market is showing a sharp split: Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Volkswagen, Nissan and other joint-venture brands are slashing ICE prices across the board — 24 of BMW's 31 models saw cuts above 10% — while BYD, Tesla, Xiaomi, NIO, XPeng, Zeekr and more than a dozen NEV brands tightened incentives or raised prices between March and May, with per-vehicle increases of RMB 2,000–20,000. Average NEV price cuts in May were just 9.6%. CPCA data show NEV retail penetration at 62.9% in May, above 60% for two consecutive months; the top 10 sales chart had zero ICE models and only four ICE nameplates remained in the top 20. Rising automotive chip costs from Infineon, STMicroelectronics and Texas Instruments are adding supply-chain pressure, marking the first industry-wide "price repair" involving 15+ brands since 2024.

## II. Autonomous Driving & Robotaxi

### 5. Waymo Recalls ~3,871 Robotaxis After Repeated Freeway Construction Zone Intrusions (Autonomous Driving / US)
**Summary:** CNBC, TechCrunch and NHTSA filings on June 18, 2026 show Waymo submitted a voluntary recall covering ~3,871 fifth-generation ADS robotaxis. Thirteen incidents occurred April–May 2026: six in Phoenix (vehicles failed to recognize ramp closure signs and entered planned construction zones) and seven in the San Francisco Bay Area (software prioritized avoiding other freeway hazards or failed to recognize zones, driving between cones into closed lanes). Waymo restricted all freeway operations from May 19 while developing an OTA fix; surface-street service continues. This is Waymo's second full-fleet recall in roughly two months after a May flood-risk recall.

**Links:**

- [CNBC — Waymo recalls about 3,900 robotaxis some drove into construction zones](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/18/waymo-nhtsa-voluntary-recall-robotaxis-entered-freeway-construction-zones.html)
