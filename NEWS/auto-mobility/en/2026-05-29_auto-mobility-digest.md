### 1. NHTSA opens preliminary probe into nearly 115,000 Rivian R1S/R1T vehicles (Regulation & safety)
**Summary:** The U.S. National Highway Traffic Safety Administration disclosed around May 29, 2026 that it opened a preliminary evaluation covering 114,922 Rivian R1S and R1T units over rear toe link assemblies that may separate while driving, causing sudden swerving.
