## IV. Mobility Platforms & Supply Chain

### 13. Massachusetts certifies first statewide US ride-hailing drivers union (Mobility)
**Summary:** PBS and the Boston Globe reported May 25–26, 2026, that Massachusetts certified the App Drivers Union to represent roughly 70,000 Uber, Lyft, and other TNC drivers after surpassing the 25% support threshold under a 2024 voter-approved sectoral bargaining law. Drivers remain independent contractors but can negotiate industry-wide pay and deactivation appeals; Uber and Lyft pledged good-faith talks, with mediation/arbitration if no deal within 180 days.

**Links:**

- [PBS News — Uber, Lyft drivers in Massachusetts certify first statewide ride-hailing union](https://www.pbs.org/newshour/nation/uber-lyft-drivers-in-massachusetts-certify-first-statewide-ride-hailing-union)
