**Summary:** On **Monday, April 27, 2026**, **NIH** issued a news release on the **OPTimize NOW** trial (also published in ***JAMA***): among infants with moderate-to-severe **neonatal opioid withdrawal syndrome (NOWS)** cared for with a family-centered **Eat, Sleep, Console (ESC)** approach, **symptom-threshold (“as-needed”) opioid dosing** versus **scheduled tapering** was associated with **~2 fewer days** to discharge readiness and **earlier medication discontinuation**; authors note the benefit pattern differed for infants initially managed with the traditional **Finnegan**-centric pathway rather than ESC.
