## III. Nutrition & Diet

### 1. Nature Communications: Phytic Acid May Support Gut Barrier via HDAC3 Pathway
**Summary:** Per mindbodygreen and Medical News Today on **June 6–10**, research in **Nature Communications** found **phytic acid (InsP6)** in whole grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds can selectively activate **HDAC3** at **10 nM**, supporting intestinal barrier integrity; **IPMK** deletion weakens the pathway and worsens permeability, partially reversible with oral InsP6. Activity is reduced in inflammatory bowel disease. Authors urge a balanced view of phytate-rich foods but caution that ordinary dietary intake alone is not yet proven therapeutic in humans.

**Links:**

- [mindbodygreen — A Study Uncovered A Potential Gut Health Benefit Of Phytic Acid (June 10, 2026)](https://www.mindbodygreen.com/articles/study-uncovered-potential-gut-health-benefit-of-phytic-acid)

**Commentary:** Adds mechanistic support for plant-forward eating — no need to over-process legumes and grains out of fear of "antinutrients" alone.
