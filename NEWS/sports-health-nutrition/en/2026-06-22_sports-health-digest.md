### 12. Circulation: childhood sugary drinks and juice linked to adult hypertension
**Summary:** Per the American Heart Association release on **June 22** in ***Circulation***, a University of Toronto team led by **Vasanti Malik** followed **25,749** U.S. children (ages **9–16**) for up to **25 years**. ≥**2** daily sugar-sweetened beverage servings raised later hypertension risk **52%** vs <3/week; each extra daily soda/sports drink added **23%**/**36%**. ≥**1.5** daily juice servings raised risk **35%**.
