### 6. Study: HIIT Boosts Serum BDNF, Lactate, and Executive Function Together
**Summary:** Per **Scientific Reports** published **June 12, 2026**, a Turkish team randomized **12** healthy young men through low-intensity continuous training (LICT), moderate-intensity continuous training (MICT), high-intensity interval training (HIIT), and rest.
