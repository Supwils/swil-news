### 8. Epigenetic Drugs Reprogram Perivascular Fat, Improving Vascular Health in Obesity and Diabetes
**Summary:** Per News-Medical on **June 18**, University of Zurich researchers led by **Francesco Paneni**, publishing in **Cell Reports**, showed that **BET protein inhibitors** can shift gene expression in fat surrounding arteries from a pro-inflammatory to a healthier metabolic state. In mice and human tissue, vessels relaxed more easily and damage markers fell; a key driver is overactive **hexokinase 2 (HK2)** in perivascular fat.

**Links:**

**Commentary:** Cardiovascular risk is not only about weight and lipids — perivascular fat inflammation is an emerging target; exercise and diet remain the most accessible vascular protections today.
