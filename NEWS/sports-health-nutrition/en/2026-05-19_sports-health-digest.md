**Summary:** WHO’s May 17, 2026 statement determines that Ebola disease caused by Bundibugyo virus in the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Uganda constitutes a Public Health Emergency of International Concern (PHEIC), while clarifying it does not meet the IHR criteria for a “pandemic emergency.” As of May 16, 2026, the statement lists eight laboratory-confirmed cases, 246 suspected cases, and 80 suspected deaths reported across at least three health zones in Ituri Province, plus travel-linked confirmed cases in Kampala, Uganda.
