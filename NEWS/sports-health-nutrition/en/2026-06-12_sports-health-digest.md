### 1. Stanford Study: Blocking "Gerozyme" 15-PGDH Regenerates Joint Cartilage in Mice and Human Samples
**Summary:** Per ScienceDaily on **June 12, 2026**, a Stanford Medicine-led **Science** study found **15-PGDH** rises in aged or injured cartilage and blocks repair; inhibiting it restored hyaline cartilage in old mice and prevented post-**ACL** osteoarthritis. Human osteoarthritis cartilage treated for **one week** showed less degradation-gene activity and new articular cartilage formation. Regeneration appears driven by reprogramming existing chondrocytes, not stem-cell proliferation; an oral **15-PGDH** inhibitor is already in Phase 1 for age-related muscle weakness, with cartilage trials hoped next.
