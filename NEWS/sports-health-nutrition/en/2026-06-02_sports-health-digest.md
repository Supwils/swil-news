## II. Health & Medicine

### 1. Post-ASCO: oral daraxonrasib ~13.2-month median survival in treated metastatic pancreatic cancer
**Summary:** Per Newsweek, NBC News, and ABC (May 31–**June 2, 2026** coverage), Revolution Medicines' **RASolute 302** Phase 3 trial (*NEJM* simultaneous publication) in 500 previously treated metastatic pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma patients showed daily oral **daraxonrasib** vs chemotherapy: **median overall survival ~13.2 vs 6.6–6.7 months** (hazard ratio ~0.40), longer progression-free survival, and lower discontinuation for toxicity; FDA plans expedited review and expanded access. Experts stress it is not a cure but a potential new standard in the refractory setting.
