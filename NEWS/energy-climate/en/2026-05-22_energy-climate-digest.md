### 1. **UN General Assembly** vote endorsing the **ICJ** climate advisory opinion: **141** in favor, **8** against, **28** abstentions; **India** abstains; **US/Russia/Saudi Arabia** among opponents (multilateral governance)
**Summary:** **UN News** (May 20, 2026) reports the General Assembly adopted a **Vanuatu**-led draft resolution on May 20 with a recorded vote of **141–8–28**.
