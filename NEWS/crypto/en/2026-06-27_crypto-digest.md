### 1. Binance lacks MiCA license; EU service limits begin in **4 days** on **July 1**
**Summary:** **Binance**, the world's largest crypto exchange, has notified users in France, Italy, Poland, Spain, and other EU jurisdictions that it will restrict new registrations and certain services from **July 1, 2026**, when the EU-wide MiCA transitional period ends, because it has not secured **CASP** authorization from any member state.
