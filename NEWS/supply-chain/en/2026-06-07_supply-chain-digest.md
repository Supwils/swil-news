## I. Policy & Trade

### 1. Section 232 metal tariff overhaul takes effect tonight: farm/HVAC cut to 15%, steel racks and aluminum plates newly at 25%
**Summary:** Per TechTimes on **June 7**, the White House **June 1** proclamation enters force at **12:01 a.m. ET on June 8**, covering roughly **$58 billion** in annual imports. Key changes include a new Annex I-C tiered rate for mobile industrial equipment (generally **25%**, **15%** for 37 framework economies, **10%** for goods with at least **85%** U.S. metal content by weight); agricultural equipment and residential HVAC moved to a **15%** total-tariff floor; and aluminum lithographic plates (HTS 3701.30.00) and steel shelving/racks (HTS 9403) newly subject to **25%** derivative duties—Global Trade Alert estimates the steel-rack addition alone adds about **$900 million** in annual duties. Net implied annual duties fall about **$3.4 billion** across affected imports; the trade-weighted average U.S. tariff moves from **10.83%** to **10.72%**. CBP issued ACE filing guidance on **June 5** (CSMS 68855869).
