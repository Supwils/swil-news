## IV. China Policy & Economy

### 8. State Council publishes Outbound Investment Regulations (Decree No. 837), effective July 1
**Summary:** According to gov.cn and China.com.cn on June 2, Premier Li Qiang signed State Council Decree No. 837 publishing the Outbound Investment Regulations, effective July 1, 2026. The 34-article rules define scope, embed a holistic national-security approach, improve overseas service systems, apply classified whole-process supervision, and strengthen investor compliance including approval, reporting, and cross-border fund registration. For discriminatory foreign restrictions on Chinese investors, the rules authorize reciprocal countermeasures and application of the Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law; authorities may also restrict foreign entities that harm China's sovereignty, security, or development interests or unfairly deprive investors of legitimate rights.
