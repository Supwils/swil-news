### 4. Shenzhou-23 crewed spacecraft launches on schedule at 23:08 Beijing time on May 24 and mission is declared a success (Science / space)
**Summary:** Xinhua reported from Jiuquan on May 24 that at 23:08 Beijing time the Shenzhou-23 spacecraft lifted off atop the Long March 2F Y23 rocket.
