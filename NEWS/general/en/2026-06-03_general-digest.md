### 11. OECD cuts 2026 global growth to 2.8%, warns prolonged Middle East war could trigger recessions
**Summary:** Per Euronews and CNA on June 3, the OECD's quarterly update lowered 2026 global growth to 2.8% from 2.9% (versus 3.4% in 2025), with 3.1% in 2027 under baseline assumptions. Asia's Middle East–dependent economies face the heaviest hit; China's growth is seen slowing from 5.0% in 2025 to 4.7% in 2026 and 4.5% in 2027.
