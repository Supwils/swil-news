### 4. Magnitude 7.8 Earthquake in Southern Philippines: At Least 37 Confirmed Dead as Rescue Continues
**Summary:** According to Rappler, Philstar, and Gulf News on June 9, a magnitude 7.8 quake struck off Sarangani province, Mindanao, in the early hours of June 8 — one of the country's strongest in 50 years. The National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (NDRRMC) updated the death toll to 37 on the morning of June 9 (33 in Soccsksargen, four in Davao), with 479 injured and four missing; the Office of Civil Defense later reported 38 deaths. Search operations continued in General Santos City and other hard-hit areas; some schools closed and airlines adjusted flights.
