Trump warned on June 28 that if the U.S. is "forced" to resume the war, "the Islamic Republic of Iran will no longer exist"; Vice President JD Vance said Washington had adhered to the ceasefire and blamed Tehran. Both sides accused the other of violating the MoU; diplomacy is near collapse though neither has formally declared negotiations over.

Al Jazeera reported some survivors accusing the government of inadequate relief, with access and disaster-zone controls still contested.
