**链接：**
- [PEGI — PEGI expands age rating criteria with interactive risk categories](https://www.pegi.info/news/pegi-expands-age-rating-criteria-interactive-risk-categories)
- [IGN — Video Games With Loot Boxes Will Now Have a 16 Age Rating Across Europe as Part of Huge PEGI Shakeup](https://www.ign.com/articles/video-games-with-loot-boxes-will-now-have-a-16-age-rating-across-europe-as-part-of-a-huge-pegi-shakeup)
**简评：** 欧洲正在把 **商业化与留存设计** 从「附加说明」升格为 **年龄门槛本身**，倒逼厂商在 **全球一套内购模板** 与 **区域合规 SKU** 之间做 **二次拆分**。
