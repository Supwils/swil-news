## II. Regulation, Regions, and Markets

### 6. India operationalizes the Promotion & Regulation of Online Gaming Rules, 2026 (from May 1), hard-banning online money games and related ads/payments
**Summary:** Reporting from **ET Now** and **The Tribune** states the **Promotion & Regulation of Online Gaming Rules, 2026** took effect on **Friday, May 1, 2026**, operationalizing the **PROG Act, 2025**. The framework bans **online money games** framed as chance, skill, or hybrid; restricts advertising and payment facilitation; and establishes institutions such as the **Online Gaming Authority of India (OGAI)** under MeitY for classification and compliance. Indian outlets also describe the industry pressing for clearer norms on classification, approvals, and enforcement timelines after commencement.
**Links:**
- [ET Now — Online Gaming Rules 2026 operationalised](https://www.etnownews.com/news/online-gaming-rules-2026-operationalised-govt-bans-real-money-games-ads-and-transactions-sets-up-regulator-registration-framework-article-154216821)
