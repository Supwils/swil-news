## III. Regulation & Compliance

### 7. PEGI From June 2026: Paid Random Items (Loot Boxes / Gacha) Minimum PEGI 16

**Summary:** Europe's PEGI system implements a new framework starting June 2026: games with paid random items (loot boxes, card packs, gacha) receive a minimum PEGI 16 rating; NFTs or unmoderated chat default to PEGI 18; time-limited offers and daily-quest engagement loops carry minimum PEGI 7 or 12 depending on design. The shift follows Germany's USK 2023 reforms, where roughly 30% of submitted titles received higher age ratings. North America's ESRB refuses to merge monetization into content ratings, keeping separate "interactive elements" labels — fueling transatlantic "rating arbitrage" debate.

**Links:**
