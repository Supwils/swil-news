### 7. PAGCOR: Philippines industry GGR down ~15.9% YoY in Q1 2026; electronic gaming segments lag (regulation / macro)
**Summary:** Inside Asian Gaming (asgam.com) on May 17, 2026 cited a weekend PAGCOR disclosure that nationwide gross gaming revenue in Q1 2026 was about Php87.6 billion (roughly US$1.42 billion), down about 15.9% year over year, with combined electronic gaming segments down about 22.4% YoY while licensed casinos regained the largest revenue share. PAGCOR chair Alejandro H. Tengco attributed pressure to Middle East geopolitical uncertainty, softer discretionary spending, and inflation.
**Links:**
- [Inside Asian Gaming — Lower E-Games sector revenue sees Philippines industry GGR fall 16% to US$1.42 billion in 1Q26](https://asgam.com/2026/05/17/lower-e-games-sector-revenue-sees-philippines-industry-ggr-fall-16-to-us1-42-billion-in-1q26/)
