### 2. Xbox "Reset" Narrative Intensifies: 3% Margin, Post-July Layoffs, Marketing Cuts

**Summary:** From June 11–13, Bloomberg, Game Developer, and others continued following CEO Asha Sharma's "Xbox Reset." In a memo with chief content officer Matt Booty, Sharma said Xbox will close the fiscal year at roughly a 3% accountability margin with nearly $500M in annual revenue decline, and that ongoing content, platform, and hardware subsidy spending "cannot continue." Layoffs are expected after the June 30 fiscal year close, with significant marketing and other budget cuts. The memo also cited storage component costs rising roughly fivefold versus two years ago and platform infrastructure "not built for the battle ahead." The Verge sources suggested studio closures or Xbox Game Studios lineup changes may follow.
