## II. Regulation, Platform Safety & Digital Consumer Rights

### 7. EU “Stop Destroying Videogames” citizens’ initiative: Commission response reportedly slated for June 16
**Summary:** The European Citizens’ Initiative “Stop Destroying Videogames” (often referred to in press as “Stop Killing Games”) gathered about 1,294,188 verified statements of support and was formally submitted to the European Commission on January 26, 2026, per the EU’s official initiative page. Euractiv reports the Commission aims to publish its formal reaction on June 16, 2026. The initiative pushes EU policymakers to address games becoming unplayable after support ends—asking for offline viability, tooling, or other safeguards so purchases do not evaporate with server sunsets.
**Links:**
- [European Citizens' Initiative — Stop Destroying Videogames](https://citizens-initiative.europa.eu/stop-destroying-videogames_en)
- [Euractiv — EU will respond to 'stop killing games' initiative in June](https://www.euractiv.com/news/eu-will-respond-to-stop-killing-games-initiative-in-june/)
