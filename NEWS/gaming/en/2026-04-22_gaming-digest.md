**Summary:** India's **Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)** published final **Online Gaming (Amendment) and Intermediary (OGAI) Rules**, effective **May 1, 2026**, tightening the regulatory framework for online real-money gaming platforms operating in India.
