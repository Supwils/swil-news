### 2. India’s Online Gaming Rules, 2026 take effect with a unified national regulator
**Summary:** Indian government-affiliated media and mainstream outlets report that rules operationalizing the Promotion and Regulation of Online Gaming Act, 2025 entered into force on May 1, 2026. The framework establishes the Online Gaming Authority of India under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) to classify games, oversee compliance, and coordinate enforcement. Coverage emphasizes a prohibition regime for online money games and related advertising and payments, alongside platform duties such as registration, age assurance, parental controls, and play-time safeguards, plus a two-tier grievance pathway and digital penalty proceedings.
**Links:**
- [News On Air — Promotion & Regulation of Online Gaming Rules, 2026 comes into force](https://www.newsonair.gov.in/promotion-regulation-of-online-gaming-rules-2026-comes-into-force/)
- [NDTV — India's New Online Gaming Act Takes Effect May 1, 2026](https://www.ndtv.com/india-news/indias-new-online-gaming-act-takes-effect-may-1-2026-key-changes-explained-11424512)
**Commentary:** Centralized oversight replaces fragmented enforcement for real-money formats while attempting to preserve lanes for compliant social titles and esports—foreign publishers must reassess distribution and payments.
