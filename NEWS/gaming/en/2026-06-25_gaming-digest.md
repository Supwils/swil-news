### 10. Curaçao Gaming Authority Issues First Crypto Compliance Guideline; Full Compliance Due by June 2027

**Summary:** Around June 25, the Curaçao Gaming Authority (CGA) issued its first detailed crypto rules for licensed online operators, effective June 2026: an immediate ban on mixers, sanctioned wallets, and "crypto-as-exchange" activity; compliant crypto policies due within three months; risk assessments, VASP due diligence, and staff training within six months; full wallet segregation, blockchain analytics, and audit-ready records by June 2027. The jurisdiction hosts a large share of global crypto casinos; the rules align offshore licensees with international AML standards.

**Links:**
