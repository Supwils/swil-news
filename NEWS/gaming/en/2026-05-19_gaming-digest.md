### 6. U.S. senators introduce the bipartisan **GAME Act** to restrict targeted sports-gambling ads to minors, with FTC enforcement
**Summary:** **Yogonet** reports Sens. **Richard Blumenthal (D-Conn.)** and **Katie Britt (R-Ala.)** introduced the **Gaming Advertisement to Minors Enforcement (GAME) Act of 2026**, proposing a nationwide ban on **targeted** digital sports-betting ads aimed at people **under 18** on large social platforms, search engines, and ad networks meeting user and revenue thresholds. The bill would place enforcement with the **Federal Trade Commission**, include escalating penalties for repeat violations, and take effect **one year after enactment** if passed as written.
