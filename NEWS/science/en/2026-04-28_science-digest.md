**Summary:** A NASA/CXC press release dated **April 28, 2026**, reports that astronomers comparing Webb fields with a deep Chandra survey identified **3DHST-AEGIS-12014**, an “**X-ray dot**” at roughly **11.8 billion light-years** that resembles the optically small, red “**little red dots**” (LRDs) seen in the early universe yet emits **X-rays**, unlike most LRDs. Institutions involved include the **Max Planck Institute for Astronomy** and **Princeton University**, among others.
