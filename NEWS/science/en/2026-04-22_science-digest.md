### 8. CMS Measurement: W Boson Mass ~80,360.2 ± 9.9 MeV Published in *Nature*
**Summary:** The **CMS experiment at CERN** published its precision measurement of the **W boson mass** as approximately **80,360.2 ± 9.9 MeV/c²** in *Nature* around April 22, 2026. The result was significant in the context of the long-running W boson mass discrepancy debate — following the **CDF collaboration's 2022 outlier result** (~80,433 MeV), the CMS measurement aligned more closely with the Standard Model prediction (~80,357 MeV), supporting the interpretation that the CDF result was a systematic measurement artifact rather than a sign of new physics.
**Links:**
