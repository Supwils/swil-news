### 5. JPL 物理学家 Turyshev 综述：在太阳系精密实验中衔接宇宙学尺度的「失联」与暗能量检验（引力与宇宙学）

**链接：**
- [ScienceDaily — NASA scientist says a mysterious "fifth force" may be hiding in our solar system](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/04/260423031528.htm)
