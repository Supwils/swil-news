**链接：**
- [WDRB / AP Technology SummaryBrief — States across the wildfire-prone Western US are using AI for early detection](https://www.wdrb.com/news/national/ap-technology-summarybrief-at-11-47-a-m-edt/article_958cfb73-6447-5b14-98a8-d71886ddcb64.html)
**简评：** **这是计算机视觉最「理直气壮」的合同场景**：误报成本高，但一旦把 **告警—复核—出勤**链路压到分钟级，**算力支出的政治阻力**远低于城市泛在监控语境。
