**链接：**
- [CNBC — AI has to be treated as a national security inflection point to fix vulnerabilities: RSAC's Easterly](https://www.cnbc.com/video/2026/05/01/ai-has-to-be-treated-as-a-national-security-inflection-point-to-fix-vulnerabilities-rsacs-easterly.html)
**简评：** 当 AI 同时降低攻击者与防御者的成本时，“安全议程”必须从工具补丁上升为跨部门的战略与投融资优先级问题。
