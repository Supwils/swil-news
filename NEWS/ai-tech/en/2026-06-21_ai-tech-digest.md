### 4. Estonia Approves **AI Agent** Digital Identity Plan, Limiting Permissions and Accountability (Policy / Europe)
**Summary:** Per **The News** on **June 21, 2026**, Estonian Prime Minister **Kristen Michal** approved an "**AI ID codes**" plan this week, positioning Estonia as the first country to regulate AI agents through a digital identity system. Users today often must grant AI assistants full access to accounts, files, and financial systems; under the new framework, agents can be authorized to "view data only," "prepare specific documents," "approve payments up to defined limits," or "operate within restricted timeframes," with all actions traceable to a responsible human. The scheme builds on existing e-Estonia infrastructure including the **X-Road** data exchange platform and digital signatures; **Michal** said it must be clear "who is acting on whose behalf, with what rights, and who is ultimately responsible."

**Links:**

- [The News — How Estonia plans to control AI agents through digital identities](https://www.thenews.com.pk/latest/1406733-how-estonia-plans-to-control-ai-agents-through-digital-identities)

**Commentary:** Estonia moves agent governance from "prompt safety" to "identity and permission engineering" — more actionable for enterprise agent rollout than abstract AI ethics.
