### 3. China's Eight Ministries Release "AI + Consumption" Guidelines With **17** Measures (Policy / China)
**Summary:** On **June 18, 2026**, China's Ministry of Commerce and seven other departments issued implementation guidelines to accelerate "AI + consumption" development, with **17** measures across five areas: smart goods consumption, service consumption, commercial innovation, promotion, and safeguards. On the goods side, the plan expands smart terminal supply, opens a humanoid-robot consumption track, builds a "people-car-home" connected ecosystem, and integrates AI with brain-computer interfaces and AR. On services, it covers home, elder care, culture and tourism, hospitality, and education — including smart nursing and rehabilitation robots in elder-care institutions. On commerce, it emphasizes smart retail, full-chain AI in e-commerce, and county-township-village smart logistics. Promotion measures include "AI + consumption" clusters and experience centers, linked to existing trade-in subsidies. The goal is to unblock structural mismatches between supply and demand and bring AI into households and retail at scale.
