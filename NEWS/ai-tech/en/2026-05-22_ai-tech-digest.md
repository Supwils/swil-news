### 1. Trump postpones AI executive order, citing fears it could erode the U.S. lead over China (U.S. policy)
**Summary:** The Associated Press and other U.S. outlets reported Thursday (around a previously expected White House signing window, with coverage continuing into May 22) that President Donald Trump decided to postpone signing an artificial intelligence–related executive order. Multiple technology outlets reported last-minute concerns raised to the White House by Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, and former adviser David Sacks, among others.
